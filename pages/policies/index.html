Policies

In this training I teach how to implement, configure, and debug the following:

 - Tripwire
 - AIDE
 - Auditd
 - AppArmor
 - SELinux
 - Sudo
 - SSH
 - PAM
 - Falco
 - Sysdig
 - Seccomp
 - Capabilities

The contents and length of the course can be adjusted by interest and relevance. Technical depth can also be adjusted based on trainees’ backgrounds.
